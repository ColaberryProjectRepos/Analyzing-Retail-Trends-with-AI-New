Methodology 3
Data Preparation
Data was cleaned, and relevant features were selected for model training. Challenges included handling large datasets and ensuring data integrity without primary key constraints. and more. Due to the large size of the dataset, it was necessary to reduce the amount of training data used for model development. 

Model Selection
Several models were tested, and the Random Forest classifier was ultimately selected based on its superior accuracy and processing efficiency. The model's performance was further optimized through adjustments in data handling and processing power. 
 

Model Development
The model development process focused on improving prediction accuracy, reducing training data size, and optimizing processing speed using the Random Forest classifier. Despite initial setbacks, the final model achieved satisfactory results.
