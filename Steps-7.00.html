Lessons Learned and Future Work 7

Challenges and Solutions

Data Quality Issues: Addressed inconsistent and incomplete data through rigorous cleaning, validation, and preprocessing techniques. 

Model Selection and Optimization: Overcame the challenge of choosing the right predictive model by employing cross-validation, hyperparameter tuning, and comprehensive performance evaluation. 

Scalability and Integration: Managed large datasets efficiently and ensured seamless integration with business systems using scalable cloud-based data warehousing and interdisciplinary collaboration.



Lessons Learned 

Ensuring high-quality data, selecting the right model, effective feature engineering, scalability, and an interdisciplinary approach are crucial for successful predictive analytics. 




Future Capabilities

Future efforts should focus on improving churn prediction models, implementing real-time analytics, advanced customer segmentation, automated reporting, and integrating with other business systems.
